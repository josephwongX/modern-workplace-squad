## The new normal is here

The transition of virtual classes and work from home have been normalized ever since the pandemic hit. The **Modern Workplace Squad** is here to improve hybrid learning classroom and employability skills by conducting workshops.

Our vision is to help people realizes their full potential and ignite positive changes in building a future to be equipped with the right mindset and tool to achieve in personal development.

Our mission is to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more by driving impact with seamless communication and collaboration across the platform and locations.
